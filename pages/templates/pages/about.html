About People Profiling

People Profiling is an open-source intelligence (OSINT) platform designed to help investigators, researchers, and security professionals uncover digital footprints, analyze behavioral data, and connect insights across multiple platforms.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become the leading intelligence tool that empowers ethical investigations, promotes transparency, and drives accountability in the digital space.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between raw social data and actionable insight by combining OSINT methodologies, digital forensics, and data visualization.

Our Values

We prioritize integrity, data ethics, and accuracy in every investigation, ensuring that digital intelligence is used responsibly and securely.
